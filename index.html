Welocme to Scrumbledore's Hogwarts

A free, online Hogwarts – themed escape room that you can play by yourself or with friends! Getting into the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry isn’t easy, and in order to get accepted, you’ll need to prove yourself. In this escape room, you’ll find yourself on your first day at the School of Wizardry. Your goal is to solve puzzles and challenges. This fun room gives you the chance to learn magic spells, and also participate in various competitions in the School of Wizardry and prove that you’re truly a witch or wizard. Don’t worry, it’s not difficult…Hints will be provided to you in case you are stuck. Use your knowledge of spells, magic, mix potions and make new friends… Are you ready to play this ultimate magical escape room and become the next Witch or Wizard of Hogwarts?? Good luck!
